⁕Amal⁕

Amal Hassan is a seventeen year old rising high school senior at Osbourn Park High School. While being devoted with her studies, she also endures in her passion for reading and journaling creative ideas that flow into her mind at any moment. Through her flow of creative ideas, she reflects on the issues that society has decided to not capture attention towards. Having endured much trouble in finding her true identity when it came to her own hair, she discovered that accepting your identity was possible through her natural hair. Through countless salon visits, products and protective styles, Amal self taught herself the mechanics of maintaining beautiful, natural hair. Wanting to save other girls from this tiring journey, naturELLE aims in assisting women with finding salons that cater to hair type and texture. Natural hair is an upcoming issue that has arisen from the darkness. NaturELLE is more than an app that allows every girl access to her hair needs, it’s allowing women of all ethnicities the freedom to embrace their natural selves. Amal is the youngest as well as the only girl in her family. She aims in pursuing her dream of being a pediatrician.

About Our App

Our app aims to allow women to find a hair salon that caters to their specfic hair type. This app displays a collection of natural hair salons in the Washington DC area based on the user's location and prefereable distance they can travel. naturELLE is an innovative app that hopes to embellsh the natural hair movement into a reality in which all women can feel beautiful by their natural beauty.
